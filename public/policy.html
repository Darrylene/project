Privacy Policy


At Brew & Blend Cafe, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

  1. Information We Collect: We collect personal information such as your name, email, and address when you place an order or sign up for our newsletter.
  2. How We Use Your Information: Your information is used solely for processing your orders, improving our services, and communicating with you about offers or updates.
  3. Data Security: We use SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We do not store your payment details on our servers.
  4. Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except for the purpose of fulfilling orders (e.g., shipping companies).
  5. Cookies: Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and track website usage.
  6. Your Rights: You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information.

For more information, please contact us at support@brewandblendcafé.com.
